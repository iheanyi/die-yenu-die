Controls:
Left/Right Arrows: Move

Up Arrow: Use (on Altars and Fireplaces)

Backstory:
You are Marik, a Demonslayer. Many moons ago, the demon Lord Yenu slaughtered your village, or most of it anyway. A great wizard, Sir Phenemon, wounded Yenu, but at the cost of his own life.
Since that day, you seek Lord Yenu's death, and you will not rest easy until his cruel soul is in your hands.
You travel to Hell's Crypt, rumored to house Lord Yenu in its deepest levels. Oh, and it's crawling with the undead. Nobody said it would be easy.
